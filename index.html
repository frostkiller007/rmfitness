Client Wins








CLIENT NO.1:

Start Weight: 83kgs
Ending Weight: 72kgs.



“ I had a shaky start to my fat loss journey, considering my unhealthy food habits, specially over the weekends. However, by tailoring my program specifically in accordance with my lifestyle and constantly feeding in my mind the importance of consistency, RM stood by me through the ups and downs of this fat loss journey. Not only did I surpass my goal and learn about how fat loss works, but also got multiple myths debunked with regards to health and nutrition. (yes, I ate 150gms rice every night) ”

CLIENT NO.2:

Start Weight: 58kgs
Ending Weight: 52kgs.



"I was strictly against dieting in any form, for the simple reason that I did not wish to compromise on my love for food. But RM made sure I ate everything I could possibly imagine. All I had to do was make sure was that my macro nutrient counts do not exceed daily limits. In terms of workouts, I used to dred going to the gym. However, with a plan comprising of exercise videos catered specifically towards my goals and requirements, not only did I start to enjoy the process but actually got hooked onto it. I could not have asked for a better coach. Thank you so much RM, this would not have been possible without you. ”



CLIENT NO.3:

"There was a complete lack of guidance with respect to fitness, and that’s when RM came in to fill the gap. Not only have I lost a significant amount of fat, but gained strength too! When you have a coach that trains you as per your pace and ability, it really motivates you and keeps you going.”
